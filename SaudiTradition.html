Saudi Arabia: Where Tradition Meets Modernity Saudi Arabia blends rich traditions with rapid modernization. It is home to landmarks like the Holy Kaaba in Mecca, Riyadh’s skyscrapers, and the vast Empty Quarter desert. Known for its Arabian hospitality and traditional dishes like Kabsa, the country’s customs reflect its deep Islamic and cultural heritage.

🎎4 Unusual Traditions in Saudi Arabia:

  • •⚔️Al-Ardha – The Saudi Sword Dance
    Al-Ardha is the national dance of Saudi Arabia, performed with swords, drums, and poetry recitals. It is a centuries-old tradition that symbolizes strength, unity, and heritage, often performed at weddings, national celebrations, and royal events.
    💡 Fun Fact:
    👑Even Saudi kings and high-ranking officials participate in Al-Ardha during important ceremonies!
  • •🎭Janadriah Festival – A Celebration of Culture
    The Janadriah Festival is one of Saudi Arabia’s biggest cultural festivals, showcasing traditional crafts, camel races, music, and folk dances. It is an annual event where Saudis and visitors experience the country’s diverse heritage.
    💡 Fun Fact:
    🏕️The festival features live reenactments of historical Saudi life, including bedouin tents, old markets, and ancient weapon demonstrations!
  • •☕Coffee Serving Etiquette – A Symbol of Hospitality
    Serving Arabic coffee (Gahwa) is a sacred tradition in Saudi culture, symbolizing hospitality and respect. It is always served in small cups without sugar, and the cup must be held in the right hand. Guests should shake the cup slightly when finished to indicate they don’t want more.
    💡 Fun Fact:
    🧑‍🦱The youngest person in the gathering is usually responsible for serving coffee to elders and guests first!
  • •🐪The Camel Beauty Pageant
    Saudi Arabia hosts the world’s largest camel beauty pageant, where camels are judged based on their size, posture, lips, and overall appearance. Winning camels can be worth millions of dollars, and owners take great pride in breeding the most beautiful ones.
    💡 Fun Fact:
    💉Some camels have been disqualified from the contest for receiving Botox injections to enhance their looks!
