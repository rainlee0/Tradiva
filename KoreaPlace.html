South Korea

Korea: A country in East Asia known for its rich culture and history. South Korea is famous for its technology, K-pop, and cuisine, while North Korea is known for its closed political system.

Capital: Seoul   Currency: Won   Continent: Asia

Places to visit

Hallasan
한라산국립공원

Scenic, volcanic, hiking

Located in Jeju Island, Hallasan is South Korea’s highest mountain. This dormant volcano features lush forests, crater lakes, and panoramic hiking trails within a protected national park.

Seoraksan
설악산국립공원

Mountainous, colorful, peaceful

Found in Gangwon Provincenear Sokcho, this stunning national park is famous for dramatic rock formations, waterfalls, and vibrant autumn foliage. Great for hiking and cable car views.

Lotte.World
롯데월드

Family-friendly, fun, magical

Located in Seoul, Lotte World is a massive theme park featuring both indoor and outdoor attractions, shopping, restaurants, and an aquarium. Ideal for families and K-pop fans!

Hahoe.Village
하회마을

Cultural, preserved, heritage

Situated in Andong, North Gyeongsang Province, this traditional village offers a glimpse into Joseon-era life with well-preserved hanok houses and cultural mask performances.
