Korea

Korea: A country in East Asia known for its rich culture and history. South Korea is famous for its technology, K-pop, and cuisine, while North Korea is known for its closed political system.

🏯4 Famous Places in South Korea

  • •🗼 Namsan Seoul Tower - 남산서울타워
    The Namsan Seoul Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in Seoul, offering a breathtaking panoramic view of the city. It’s also called the "Love Tower," where visitors place love locks on the fence as a symbol of their relationships.
    💡 Fun Fact:
    🌈At night, the tower lights up in different colors, which indicate the air quality in the city!
  • •🏡 Bukchon Hanok Village - 북촌한옥마을
    The Bukchon Hanok Village is one of the most charming places in Seoul, featuring traditional Korean houses (hanok) that date back hundreds of years. Walking through this area feels like stepping back in time!
    💡 Fun Fact:
    🤫Many of these houses are still inhabited, so visitors are encouraged to walk quietly to respect the local residents!
  • •🏝️ Jeju Island - 제주도
    Jeju Island is famous for its breathtaking natural beauty, featuring waterfalls, stunning beaches, and dormant volcanoes. It’s one of Korea’s natural wonders and a top destination for romantic getaways!
    💡 Fun Fact:
    🧜‍♀️The island is home to the famous "Haenyeo" (women divers), who are skilled in free diving to collect seafood without modern diving equipment!
  • •🏯 Gyeongbokgung Palace - 경복궁
    The Gyeongbokgung Palace is the largest and oldest royal palace in South Korea, where the kings of the Joseon Dynasty ruled. The palace features beautiful gardens and stunning traditional halls.
    💡 Fun Fact:
    👘Visitors can rent traditional Korean clothing (hanbok - 한복) and enter the palace for free!
